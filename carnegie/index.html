MORE for EU Citizens

Mobility and Political Rights Enhancement for EU Citizens aims to address the issues facing mobile EU citizens

  • Francesco Siccardi

particularly those related to their rights to family reunification and political participation.

The ultimate goal is to improve the enforcement of EU citizens' rights by the EU institutions, raise EU citizens' awareness of their EU rights and engage national policy makers in discussing the obstacles facing mobile EU citizens.

  • , Ken Godfrey
  • , Ruth-Marie Henckes
  • , Erin Jones
  • , Elisa Lledó
  • , Kinga Brudzinska

The aim of the Improving Participation of EU Citizens (IPEU) project is to promote the political participation of mobile citizens in the EU and increase the capacity and knowledge of civil servants and relevant national, regional and local experts on EU citizenship and related political rights.

TI-EU

Transnational Interaction of EU Citizens

  • Sinan Ülgen

Transnational Interaction of EU Citizens (TI-EU) is a program specially developed to enable interaction between EU citizens of the different Member States. Trips are organized for European citizens to the various European countries, with an emphasis on interaction between European citizens.

FAIR EU

The FAIR EU

  • Olivia Lazard

The FAIR EU (Fostering Awareness, Inclusion and Recognition of EU Citizens) project aims to promote the successful integration of EU citizens into the civic and political life of their host countries through a holistic approach to the obstacles they face in exercising their rights.
